SPICY NOODLES

ingredients

Noodles: Wheat Flour (sourced from USA), Salt, Water. Sauce Packet: Soy Sauce (Soya Beans, Water, Salt, Sugar), Chili Oil, White Sesame Oil (Soy Oil, Sesame Oil). Vegetable Packet: Dried Cabbage, Dried Carrot, Dried Mushroom, Dried Fungus, Dried Green Onion.

NUTRITIONAL FACTS

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

1. REMOVE THE NOODLES & VEGETABLES FROM THE PACKAGING AND ADD THESE CONTENTS INTO THE MICROWAVEABLE TRAY. DO NOT ADD THE SAUCE PACKET YET.

2. ADD WATER TO THE FILL LINE, COVERING THE NOODLES. MICROWAVE WITHOUT THE LID ON HIGH FOR 4 MINUTES, OR UNTIL NOODLES ARE COOKED.

3. Once the noodles are cooked, use the lid to cover the tray and drain the water (CAUTION: HOT WATER).

4. REMOVE THE LID. ADD THE SAUCE AND MIX UNTIL
EVENLY DISTRIBUTED.

enjoy!

COMPLETELY
AIR-DRIED AND
NON-FRIED.
